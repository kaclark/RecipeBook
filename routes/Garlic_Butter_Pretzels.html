Instructions


1.) Preheat oven 400F

2.) Line Sheet pan with parchment paper

3.) Microwave butter for 45s

4.) Add Seasonings

5.) Stir butter and seasoning

6.) Lay pretzels out on tray

7.) Pour Butter Garlic Sauce on Pretzels

8.) Bake in oven for 7min
