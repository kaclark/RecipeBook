Chicken Noodle Pan

Equipment


Pan

Utensil

Cutting Board

Knife

Pot

Strainer

Ingredients


Umami contact Base(e.g. Chicken Breast or Boneless Skinless Thigh, Mushroom, Tofu)

Chopped Onion

Garlic(the fresher/closer to natural form the better)

Sauce of choice

Vegetable of Choice (maybe go green and leafy? Brocoli counts)

Dumpling

Instructions


1.) Wash Vegetable

2.) Boil pot of water

3.) Add Vegetable to Pan in one corner or side

4.) Add Dumplings to Pan in another corner or side

5.) In the remaining space add garlic, chopped onion, and umami base

6.) Add oil to the umami base

7.) Add water to pan to lightly submerge the dumplings and vegetable

8.) Allow water to come in contact with the umami base if it happens

9.) Cover the pan and steam on medium heat for 7-11 minutes

10.) Add Noodles to pot of water

11.) Cook Noodles for 3 minutes

12.) Strain Noodles and keep to the side

13.) Uncover and set dumplings to the side

14.) Add Noodles to Umami base and mix

15.) Add Sauce of choice used in Umami base to Noodles

16.) Raise heat and evaporate off excess water

17.) Only Sauce should remain; no water

18.) Add dumplings back in with just a bit of oil

19.) Gently fry dumplings for 2 minutes

20.) Warm Pita bread and cut into 8 slices

21.) Add Pita to pan

22.) Serve and Enjoy
