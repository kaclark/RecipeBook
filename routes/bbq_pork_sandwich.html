Bbq Pork Sandwich

[Return to Integrations Hub]

Equipment


Cutting Board

Knife

Frying Pan

Utensil

Spatula

Ingredients


Precooked Cubed Pork

Hickory Sauce

Olive Oil

Bun

Cheese Slice

Sun Dried Tomatoes

Kewpie Mayo

Instructions


1.) Med Heat Oil in Pan

2.) Chop cubed pork

3.) Add Pork to Pan

4.) Air Fry Bun for 5m 400F

5.) Stir Pork

6.) Add Hickory Sauce

7.) Wait for Bun

8.) Lower Heat of Pan

9.) Add Cheese to Pork

10.) Spread Mayo on Bun

11.) Add Sun Dried Tomatoes to Bun

12.) Use Spatula to scoop Cheese and Pork

13.) Add Pork and Cheese to Bun
