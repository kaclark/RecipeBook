Easy Italian Baked Chicken

Equipment


Oven

Baking Sheet

Mixing Bowl

Small Bowl

Small Plate

Spoon x 2

Sharp knife or cheese grater

(Optional) Tin Foil or Parchment Paper

Ingredients


Thin-sliced chicken cutlets

Italian / pre-seasoned breadcrumbs

Eggs

Parmesan cheese wedge

Garlic powder

Olive oil or cooking spray

Instructions


1.) Add eggs to mixing bowl, whisk with spoon until uniform

2.) Add a touch of olive oil to the mixing bowl, set aside

3.) Grate or cut parmesan cheese onto plate

4.) Add breadcrumbs and garlic powder, mix until uniform, set aside

5.) Preheat oven to 400F

6.) (Optional) line baking pan

7.) Lightly brush olive oil onto pan or use cooking spray

8.) Dip chicken into egg/oil wash

9.) Bread chicken, adding more breadcrumbs/garlic as needed

10.) Place as much chicken onto pan as possible

11.) Bake for 15min uninterrupted

12.) Ensure thickest cutlet has cooked through, serve warm
