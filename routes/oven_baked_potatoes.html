Oven Baked Potatoes

Equipment


Oven

Sheet pan

Tin foil or parchement paper

Cutting board

Knife

Strainer

Ingredients


Olive Oil

Salt

Pepper

Onion Powder

Garlic Powder

Herbal/Italian Season blend

Instructions


1.) Preheat oven to 400F

2.) Line sheet pan

3.) Wash potatoes in strainer

4.) Cut potatoes into small square chunks

5.) Add potatoes to pan

6.) Pour oil all over potatoes, jostle them around to coat in oil

7.) Season potatoes

8.) Cook potatoes for 30-40min; should be soft
