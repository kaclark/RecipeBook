Balsamic Jalapeno Flatbread

[Return to Integrations Hub]

Equipment


Sheet Pan

Frying Pan

Cutting Board

Small Bowl

Knife

Ingredients


Balsamic Vinegar

Jalapeno Flatbread

Instructions


1.) Heat oven to 350F

2.) Place Bread on Sheetpan

3.) Cook Bread in oven 12m

4.) Heat Balsamic in Pan low-med heat

5.) Wait 10 min, pour reduction in bowl

6.) Cut Bread on Cutting Board
