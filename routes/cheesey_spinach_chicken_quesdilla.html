cheesey_spinach_chicken_quesdilla

Equipment


Frying Pan

Cutting Board

Knife

Bowl

Ingredients


Chicken Breast

Spinach

Shredded Cheese

Chopped Onions

Olive Oil

Salt

Pepper

Assorted Seasonings

Instructions


1.) Add Oil to pan, heat on med-low

2.) Add Chopped Onions to Pan

3.) Add Salt to Onions

4.) Add Pepper to Onions

5.) Cut Chicken into small chunks; the smaller the better

6.) Add Chicken to Pan

7.) Add Salt and Pepper to Chicken

8.) Add Assorted Seasonings to Chicken

9.) Cook for 8 minutes on medium heat(+2 min for larger chunks)

10.) Add Spinach to Pan

11.) Cook for 2-4 minutes

12.) Transfer pan fry to bowl

13.) Clean pan

14.) Heat dry pan on low

15.) Add tortilla to pan

16.) Layer Shredded Cheese, wait until melted

17.) Add pan fry to half of tortilla

18.) Fold Tortilla in half

19.) Smash Tortilla

20.) Repeat for each tortilla or until pan fry is exhausted
