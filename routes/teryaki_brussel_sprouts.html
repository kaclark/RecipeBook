Teryaki Brussel Sprouts

Equipment


Air Fryer

Cutting Board

Knife

Bowl

Air Fryer Liner

Ingredients


Brussel Sprouts

Garlic

Salt

Pepper

Teryaki with Seasame and Ginger

MSG

Crushed Red Pepper

Avacado Spray Oil

Instructions


1.) Wash Brussel Sprouts

2.) Cut Brussel Sprouts in half

3.) Line Bowl with Air Fryer Liner

4.) Toss Brussel Sprouts in bowl

5.) Drip Teryaki Sauce onto each brussel sprout

6.) Mix

7.) Add Salt to bowl

8.) Add Pepper to Bowl

9.) Mix

10.) Add Crushed Red Pepper

11.) Add MSG

12.) Peel and grate garlic into bowl

13.) Move Liner from Bowl to Air Fryer

14.) Spray brussel sprouts with oil

15.) Cook at 400F for 15min
