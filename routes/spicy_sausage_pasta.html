spicy sausage pasta

Equipment


Pan

Flat container

Utensil

Cutting Board

Knife

Ingredients


Linguine

Pasta Sauce

Sausage

Olive Oil

Salt

Pepper

Cayenne Pepper

Garlic Powder

Onion Powder

Parsley

Instructions


1.) Place dry Linguine in flat container

2.) Cover Linguine with cold water

3.) Let Linguine sit in cold water for 2 hours

4.) Add oil to pan, heat on medium-low

5.) Season Oil

6.) Cut Sausage into small chunks

7.) Add Sausage to pan

8.) Add Salt and Pepper to Sausage

9.) Saute for 10 minutes

10.) Add sauce and cook for 3 minutes

11.) Add hydrated Pasta to pan and cook for 1 additional minute

12.) Serve
