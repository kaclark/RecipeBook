oven baked chicken drumsticks

Equipment


Oven

Pan

Tin Foil or Parchment Paper

Mixing Bowl

Ingredients


Chicken Drumsticks

Seasame Oil

Crushed Red Pepper

Salt

Pepper

Onion Powder

Garlic Powder

Instructions


1.) Put Chicken in bowl

2.) Add Seasame Oil to bowl

3.) Add copious amounts of salt, pepper, onion, and garlic powder

4.) (Optional) Marinate for hours

5.) Preheat oven to 400F(Do first if skipping marination)

6.) Line baking pan

7.) Arrange Drumsticks, alternating up and down orientation

8.) Douse with crushed red pepper

9.) Bake for 40min(20 min, flip, 20 min)

10.) Ensure temperature of test drumstick has exceeded 165F
