chicken_tikka_masala

Equipment


Pan

Can Opener

Utensil

Cutting Board

Knife

Ingredients


Chicken Breast

Canned Crushed Tomatoes

Garlic

Garam Masala

Cumin

Salt

Sugar

Onion

Paprika

Cayenne Pepper

Instructions


1.) Peel, Chop garlic cloves

2.) Add garlic to bowl

3.) Cut Chicken

4.) Add Chicken to bowl

5.) Add Plain Yogurt

6.) Add Garam Masala, Salt, Cumin, Paprika, Ginger powder to bowl

7.) Mix

8.) Marinate for 1-30 hours

9.) Chop onion

10.) Heat pan with oil

11.) Sear Chicken in pan

12.) Place chicken in bowl

13.) Add onions to pan, fry a bit

14.) Add Sugar, Ginger, Cayenne, Garam Masala

15.) Add Crushed Tomatoes

16.) Cook for 5 min

17.) Optional: blend

18.) Add Chicken

19.) Cook for 10 minutes

20.) Add whipping cream

21.) Cook for 7 minutes
