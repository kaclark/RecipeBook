Instructions


1.) Preheat Oven 400F

2.) Put Feta in Dutch Oven

3.) Wash Tomatoes with Colander

4.) Put tomatoes in Dutch Oven

5.) Add Salt, Garlic, Onion, Pepper Flake to Dutch Oven

6.) Add olive oil to dutch oven

7.) Put Dutch Oven in Oven 40m

8.) Boil Barilla

9.) Take Dutch Oven out of Oven

10.) Strain Barilla with Colander

11.) Add Barilla to Dutch Oven

12.) Stir Pasta into Sauce
