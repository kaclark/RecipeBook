Mushroom Beef Sandwich

[Return to Integrations Hub]

Equipment


Oven

Sheet Pans

Frying Pan

Utensil

Ziplock Bag

Clingwrap

Ingredients


Sliced Sourdough Bread

Herbs and Spices

Avacado Oil

Roast Beef

*Thai Mushroom

ColbyJack Cheese

Spring Mix

Kewpie Mayo/Balsamic

Instructions


1.) Preheat Oven 400F

2.) Lie Bread out on Pan

3.) Spray bread with oil

4.) Sprinkle Herb on bread

5.) Sprinkle Garlic on bread

6.) Sprinkle Salt on bread

7.) Add Cheese to bread slice

8.) Add Meat to same slice

9.) Leave other slice empty

10.) Put Pan in Oven for 6m

11.) Pan Fry Mushroom for 5m

12.) Take Pan out of oven

13.) Put spring mix on empty slice

14.) Add Mayo/Balsamic to greens

15.) Top greens with Mushroom

16.) Put bread slices together

17.) Wrap and Bag Sandwich
