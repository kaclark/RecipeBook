Sesame Noodles

Equipment


Fridge

Container

Cutting Board

Knife

Microplane

Pot

Frying Pan

Tongs

Strainer

Spatula

Ingredients


Kewpie Sesame Marinade

Protein Base(eg Chicken, Tofu)

Onion

Garlic

Crushed Red Pepper

Vinegar

Olive Oil

Chicken/Vegetable Stock

Shaoxing Cooking Wine

Soy Sauce

MSG

Salt

Pepper

Noodles

Stir Fry Vegetables

Instructions


1.) Chop onion

2.) Add onion to container

3.) Peel Garlic

4.) Process Garlic through microplane into container

5.) Chop stir fry vegetables, add to container

6.) Chop protein base, add to container

7.) Remove cutting board

8.) Add 4 tsp crushed red pepper to container

9.) Add 3 tsp oil to container

10.) Add 2 tsp vinegar to container

11.) Add 1/3 cup of Kewpie Sesame Marinade to container

12.) Add 4 tsp Soy Sauce to container

13.) Mix container

14.) Refridgerate Container for 30min-48hrs

15.) Set water in pot to boil

16.) Stir Fry Containter contents for 12 minutes

17.) Add 1/3 cup of Shaoxing Wine

18.) Let simmer on med heat for 2 min

19.) Add 1/3 cup chicken stock

20.) Boil Noodles for 3 minutes

21.) Add 1/2 cup of Noodle water to pan

22.) Strain Noodles

23.) Add Noodles to pan

24.) Simmer Noodles in stir fry for 2 minutes

25.) Serve
