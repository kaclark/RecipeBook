Sesame Noodles

Equipment


Fridge

Container

Cutting Board

Knife

Microplane

Pot

Frying Pan

Tongs

Strainer

Spatula

Ingredients


Kewpie Sesame Marinade

Protein Base(eg Chicken, Tofu)

Onion

Garlic

Crushed Red Pepper

Vinegar

Olive Oil

Chicken/Vegetable Stock

Shaoxing Cooking Wine

Soy Sauce

MSG

Salt

Pepper

Noodles

Stir Fry Vegetables

Instructions


1.) Chop onion

2.) Add onion to container

3.) Peel Garlic

4.) Process Garlic through microplane into container

5.) Chop stir fry vegetables, add to container

6.) Chop protein base, add to container

7.) Remove cutting board

8.) Add 4 tsp crushed red pepper to container

9.) Add 2 tsp salt to container

10.) Add 3 tsp oil to container

11.) Add 2 tsp vinegar to container

12.) Add 1/3 cup of Kewpie Sesame Marinade to container

13.) Add 4 tsp Soy Sauce to container

14.) Mix container

15.) Refridgerate Container for 30min-48hrs

16.) Set water in pot to boil

17.) Stir Fry Containter contents for 12 minutes

18.) Crack Black Pepper into pan

19.) Add 6 tsp MSG

20.) Add 1/3 cup of Shaoxing Wine

21.) Let simmer on med heat for 2 min

22.) Add 1/3 cup chicken stock

23.) Boil Noodles for 3 minutes

24.) Add 1/2 cup of Noodle water to pan

25.) Strain Noodles

26.) Add Noodles to pan

27.) Simmer Noodles in stir fry for 2 minutes

28.) Serve
