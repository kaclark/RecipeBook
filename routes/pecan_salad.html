Pecan Salad

Equipment


Tongs

Bowl

Tablespoon

Half Tablespoon

Ingredients


Spring Mix

Roasted Pecan Pieces

Avacado Spray Oil

White Wine Vinegar

Salt

Pepper

Crushed Red Pepper

MSG

Instructions


1.) Place Spring Mix in Bowl

2.) Spray Spring Mix with Spray Oil

3.) Toss Spring Mix with Tongs

4.) Spray again

5.) Pour 1 tsp vinegar over Spring Mix

6.) Toss Spring Mix

7.) Add fourth tsp of Salt to Spring Mix

8.) Toss

9.) Add fourth tsp Pepper to Spring Mix

10.) Toss

11.) Add fourth tsp Crushed Red Pepper to Spring Mix

12.) Toss

13.) Add fourth tsp MSG to Spring Mix

14.) Toss

15.) Top with handful of roasted pecan fragments
