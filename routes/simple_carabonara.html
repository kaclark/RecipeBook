Simple Carabonara

Equipment


Frying Pan

Stirring utensil

Bowl

Measuring cup

Strainer

Ingredients


Eggs

Olive oil

Parmesan

Crushed Red Pepper

Garlic Powde

pasta

onion salt

Pepper

Instructions


1.) Cook pasta according to package instructions

2.) In Pan, Heat oil with crushed red pepper, garlic, onion, salt. Stir to distribute flavor in fat

3.) Mix 4 eggs and Parmesan in bowl.

4.) Retain 1 cup of pasta water then strain pasta

5.) Add pasta to Pan

6.) Pour egg and cheese into Pan

7.) Create sauce by stirring and mixing pasta while adding small amounts of pasta water to Pan

8.) Serve
