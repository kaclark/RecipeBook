Pan Fried Potatoes

Equipment


Pan

Utensil

Stove

Cutting Board

Knife

Strainer

Tablespoon

Ingredients


Peanut Oil

Salt

Pepper

Chopped Onion

Minced Garlic

Instructions


1.) Heat stove to medium heat

2.) Put 3 tbs of Peanut Oil into the pan

3.) Add chopped Onions to pan

4.) Salt chopped onions

5.) Wash potatoes in strainer

6.) Cut potatoes into small square chunks

7.) Add potatoes to pan

8.) Add salt and pepper to the potatoes

9.) Add a tablesppon of micned garlic

10.) Cook potatoes for 15-20min
