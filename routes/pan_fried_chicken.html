Pan Fried Chicken

Equipment


Pan

Stove

Cutting Board

Knife

Table Spoon

Utensil

Ingredients


1lb Chicken

Salt

Pepper

Crushed Red Pepper

Chopped Onions

Peanut Oil

Instructions


1.) Heat Stove to Medium

2.) Add 3 Tbs of Peanut Oil to Pan

3.) Add chopped onions to pan

4.) Salt Onions

5.) Cut Chicken into small chunks

6.) Add Chicken to Pan

7.) Season Chicken with Salt, Pepper, and Crushed Red Pepper

8.) Stir Fry for 10-15min. Ensure a test chicken chunk exceeds temperature of 165F
