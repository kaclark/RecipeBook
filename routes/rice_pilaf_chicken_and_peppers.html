Instructions


1.) heat Pan(&E) on medium

2.) add onions* and oil

3.) add "crushed red pepper"(&I) and "garlic powder"(&I) to Pan(&E)

4.) let fry for 2 min

5.) stir with Utensil(&E)

6.) add peppers* to Pan(&E)

7.) let fry for 5 min

8.) add chicken* to Pan(&E)

9.) let fry for 8 min; stir with Utensil(&E) occasionally

10.) add rice* to Pan(&E)

11.) stir with Utensil(&E) and let fry for 3 min
