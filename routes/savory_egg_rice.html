Savory Egg Rice

Equipment


Frying Pan

Sheet Pan

Bowls

Knife

Cutting Board

Pot

Ingredients


Boxed Rice Pilaf

Boneless Chicken Thighs

Green Onions

Fresh Parsley

3 Eggs

Olive Oil

Salt

Pepper

Seasoning

Chili Pepper/Cayenne Pepper

Crushed Red Pepper

Instructions


1.) Add rice, rice-seasoning, and 1.75 cups of water to frying pan

2.) Boil

3.) Lower heat to low and simmer while covered for 20 minutes

4.) Cut Green Onions, store in bowl

5.) Cut Fresh Parsley, store in bowl

6.) Crack 3 Eggs in bowl

7.) Seperate Egg Yolk from Egg White, store seperately

8.) Dice Chicken Thigh

9.) Transfer Rice to sheet pan

10.) Pre-heat Oven to 400F

11.) Dry Rice for 5 minutes

12.) Heat frying pan with oil

13.) Add Chicken to pan

14.) Add Salt and Pepper to Pan

15.) Add Seasonings to Pan

16.) Add Cayenne Pepper to pan

17.) Cook for 8 minutes

18.) Transfer Rice to Bowl

19.) Add Egg Yolk to Rice and stir until yolk is diffuse

20.) Add Rice to pan

21.) Add Crushed Red Pepper to Rice

22.) Cook for 4 minutes

23.) Move rice and chicken to side of pan to create a clearing in the middle

24.) Pour in egg white

25.) Stir egg white into frying rice

26.) Add Chopped Green Onion

27.) Mix and Cook for 2 minutes

28.) Add Parsely

29.) Serve
