cheesy spinach scrambled eggs

Equipment


Bowl

Pan

Utensil

Ingredients


Spinach

Eggs

Olive Oil

Seasonings

Salt

Pepper

Cheese

Instructions


1.) Heat pan to medium low

2.) Crack and Mix Eggs with seasoning, salt, and pepper

3.) Sautee Spinach

4.) Add Salt to Pan

5.) Add mixed eggs to pan

6.) Scramble for 10 minutes on low heat

7.) Scramble for 3 minutes on medium high heat to finish solidifying eggs

8.) Serve
